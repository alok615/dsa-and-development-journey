## Learning Philosophy

> *"Consistency beats intensity. Showing up every day, even when it is hard, is what separates the prepared from the unprepared."*

- **Daily practice** over cramming sessions
- **Understanding approaches** before memorizing solutions
- **Documenting everything** to reinforce learning and help others
- **Quality over quantity** - deep understanding of fewer problems beats shallow understanding of many
- **Building in public** to stay accountable and demonstrate growth
